All American Apple Pie

Nothing hits your tastebuds like this good old american favorite. Wether its hot, cold, with ice cream, or without, the apple pie is a cornerstone in the pieman's tasty repertoire.

The Main Ingredient

Apples, apples, and more apples! Sweet, tart, or in between, there are so many you can choose. Here is the Pieman's top 5 for making that good ol mouth watering pie!

  1. Empire (slightly tart)
  2. Courtland (slightly tart)
  3. Gala (sweet)
  4. Golden Delicious (sweet)
  5. Granny Smith (tart)

The Crust of it all

What's a perfect pie without a perfect piecrust? There are 4 basic types of pie crusts.

  1. Flakey
  2. Sweet
  3. Crumb
  4. Mealy

Typically the homemade apple pie uses a flakey crust topped in a cross hatch pattern or covered with cutouts inthe shape of leaves or other type of ornamental shapes. However there is more to our crust than the name alone. The type of flour, and fatting that you use will have a great impact on wether your pie is a golden delight or a messy brown flop. Pieman Factoids

Apple pie recipes can be dated back to the 1300's. It's popularity in american pop culture using the phrase "for Mom and apple pie" supposedly originated from a WWII soldier's answer for why they were going to war.
