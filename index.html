All American Apple Pie

Nothing hits your tastebuds like this good old american favorite. Whether its hot, cold, with ice cream, or without, the apple pie is a cornerstone in the PieMan's tasty repertoire.

The Main Ingredient

Apples, apples, and more apples! Sweet, tart, or in between, there are so many you can choose. Here is the PieMan's top 5 for making that good ol mouth watering pie!

  1. Empire (slightly tart)
  2. Courtland (slightly tart)
  3. Gala (sweet)
  4. Golden Delicious (sweet)
  5. Granny Smith (tart)

The Crust of it All

What's a perfect pie without a perfect piecrust? There are 4 basic types of pie crusts.

  1. Flakey
  2. Sweet
  3. Crumb
  4. Mealy

Typically the homemade apple pie uses a flakey crust topped in a cross hatch pattern. Sometimes it is covered with cutouts in the shape of leaves or other type of ornamental shapes. However there is more to our crust than the name alone. The type of flour, and fat (butter, lard, vegetable shortning) that you use will have a great impact on whether your pie is a golden delight or a messy brown flop. Other ingredients like salt, sugar, eggs, and the liquid in the filling also go a long way to make the pie crust a success. Pieman Factoids
